# Equalizer sound.

The *Energy Tower 3* has two controllers on its rear side to adjust the EQ sound.

* Treble EQ ().
* Bass EQ ().

You can also select 1 of the 5 preset equalizers by pressing the (-) button on the remote control:

* EQ 0: Normal.
* EQ 1: Pop.
* EQ 2: Rock.
* EQ 3: Jazz.
* EQ 4: Classic.
