## Main Functions

### <quick_guide> Turn on / off your device

Use the button (4) to turn on / off your device.

To turn on your device, press and keep 2 seconds the ON/OFF button (4). The DISPLAY (8) will turn on; first the heart icon and then the screen, meaning that the device is now turned on.

To turn off your device, press and keep 2 seconds the ON/OFF button (4).


### Changing play mode

When you turn the device, Audio-In mode will start automatically. Connect the audio cable (included) to the AUDIO-IN connection (13) of the *Energy Music Box Z6* to use the speaker function. Press the MODE button (3) to switch between different playback modes.


</quick_guide>

### Charging the battery

When the speaker stop emit sound or the battery icon start blinking, is the time to charge it. Connect the end of the included USB cable to your *Energy Music Box Z6* (10) and the other end to any USB port that can provide energy (PC, USB wall charger, car, etc). The LED INDICATOR (9) will turn in red color and when the charge is completed the LED (9) will turn off.

**Note:** It is recommended to turn off the device when charging. Do not store or leave unused for a long period of time with the battery completely discharged.
