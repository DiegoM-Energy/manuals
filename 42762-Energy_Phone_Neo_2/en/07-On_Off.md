## <quick_guide> Switch on and off

Make sure that the microSIM card or both cards and the battery (19) are inserted in their corresponding slots before turning on your Energy Phone Neo 2. Press the On / Off button (10) on the right of your device for one second. Once the operating system has loaded completely, please unlock the device to start using it. Proceed to enter the PIN number of your microSIM cards and then drag the padlock upwards.


To turn off the device, press the On / Off button (10) until you see the power off menu and then, select "Switch off".

Briefly press the On / Off button (10) to lock/unlock the device thus, turning the screen on/off.



</quick_guide>
