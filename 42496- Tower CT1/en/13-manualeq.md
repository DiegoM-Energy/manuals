## Sound equalizer

The device has two controllers at the rear by which you can equalize sound:
*	Mid and Treble adjustment.
*	Bass adjustment.

You can also select 1 of the 5 preset equalizers, by pressing the (EQ) remote control key:
*	EQ 0: Normal
*	EQ 1: Pop
*	EQ 2: Rock
*	EQ 3: Jazz
*	EQ 4: Classic
*	EQ 5: Country
*	EQ 5: Bass
