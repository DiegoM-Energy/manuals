## <quick_guide> Bluetooth Pairing
Everytime you turn on your *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1* it will enter into pairing mode automatically; at the same time start the search and pairing process from your BluetoothÂ® device (smartphone, tablet, laptop, etc). Once you have found the *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1* in your device, select "Connect" and both devices will pair each other immediately with no need of pairing codes; the speaker will play a sound indicating that the connection has been stablished. 

To pair again with the last device paired it is enought to turn on the *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1* and the synchronization will take place automatically.
