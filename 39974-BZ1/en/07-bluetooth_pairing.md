## <quick_guide> Bluetooth Pairing
Everytime you turn on your *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1*, pairing mode will start automatically; at the same time, the search and pairing process will start on your BluetoothÂ® device (smartphone, tablet, laptop, etc). Once you have identified the *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1* on your device, select "Connect" and both devices will pair each other immediately with no need of pairing codes; the speaker will play a sound indicating that the connection has been established. 

To pair the speaker with the last device again, you only need to turn on the *Energy Bluetooth Music Box BZ1* and the synchronization will take place automatically.
