## Key Features
* 7" TFT screen 16:9 (800x480 pixels).
* 5 points capacitive multitouch screen.
* Dual Core ARM Cortex A7 1.5GHz processor and Mali 400 GPU.
* 512MB RAM.
* 4GB GB internal memory expandable memory up to 64GB via microSD-HC/XC cards.
* Wi-Fi connection (802.11 b/g/n).
* Bluetooth 4.0.
* Built-in front camera VGA.
* Built-in rear camera VGA.
* USB-Host.
* USB-OTG .
* Built-in speaker 1w.
* Built-in omnidirectional microphone.
* Motion sensor (accelerometer).
* Battery capacity: 3.7v/2.200mAh.
* AC/DC adapter 100-240v 50-60Hz DC 5V/2A.
