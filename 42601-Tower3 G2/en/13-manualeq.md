## Sound equalizer

This device has two controllers at the back to adjust equalization:

* Adjust Mid/Treble.
* Adjust Bass.

You can also select 1 of the 5 preset equalizers by pressing the (EQ) button on the remote control:

* EQ 0: Normal.
* EQ 1: Pop.
* EQ 2: Rock.
* EQ 3: Jazz.
* EQ 4: Classic.
